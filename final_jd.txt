### Job Title: Data Engineer

**Overview**  
Are you passionate about transforming data into actionable insights? Join our dynamic team as a Data Engineer, where you will play a pivotal role in building scalable data solutions that drive informed decision-making. We value collaboration, innovation, and a commitment to excellence in everything we do. In this role, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure our data architecture supports our business objectives and enhances our analytical capabilities.

**Responsibilities**  
- **Design, Implement, and Optimize ETL Processes:** Create and refine ETL processes to ensure efficient data flow and transformation, enabling seamless access to high-quality data.
- **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Engage with stakeholders to gather and understand data requirements, delivering tailored data solutions that meet their needs and drive business value.
- **Data Pipeline Development:** Develop and maintain robust data pipelines using technologies such as SQL, Spark, and Python, ensuring they are scalable and efficient.
- **MLOps Practices:** Leverage MLOps methodologies to ensure the smooth deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of machine learning models, enhancing our data-driven decision-making capabilities.
- **Clinical Data Management:** Work with clinical data to support analytics and reporting initiatives, ensuring compliance with relevant regulations and best practices.
- **Troubleshooting and Data Quality Assurance:** Proactively troubleshoot and resolve data processing issues, maintaining high standards of data quality and reliability.
- **Continuous Learning:** Stay current with industry trends and emerging technologies, continuously improving your skills and knowledge to drive innovation within the team.
